A Quick Bio

I've been fascinated by tools and technology as long as I can remember. My father fed my interests with subscriptions to magazines like Popular Science, which I devoured every month, thrilled by the mind-blowing advance of technology and the rise of Tesla.

Eager to grow up, I got my first job at a bicycle retail store at 14 years old. Here I discovered a life of learning under direct mentors, working with my hands, and providing creative technical solutions that improve lives. There is no better feeling to me than receiving the gratitude and trust of someone to whom I've provided a real and personalized service.

But this is not limited to face-to-face connections. I had the same gratitude and trust for the quality parts distributors and manufacturer warranty offices that I worked with on a daily basis, and I learned the value of businesses that put their clients first and invest in their technology.

In 2020, after seven years as a bicycle mechanic, I decided to go to App Academy and finally learn to be a software engineer. I'm thrilled to have the skills I've wanted my whole life, and I cannot wait to dedicate my time and effort to building the incredible technology that will inspire kids like me.
